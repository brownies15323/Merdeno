The Grand Churrasco Buffet Dinner (FRI-SAT)

Welcome to Merdeno

Experience the ultimate The Grand Churrasco Buffet Dinner. Indulge in over 16 premium-grade cuts of beef, expertly prepared Brazilian-style by our Brazilian chefs. Our unique Rodizio service, ensuring a limitless and timeless dining experience. Explore a wide range of hot dishes, side salads, premium cold cut and seafood station with dessert station only at Pedro Churrascaria and Steakhouse, Novotel Bangkok Future Park Rangsit.

Skewered menus (served in rodizio style)

  • Picanha: The prime part of the sirloin seasoned with sea salt and served thin-sliced
  • Garlic Picanha: Elevate the classic Picanha with a rich infusion of garlic flavor.
  • Garlic Steak: Sirloin beef seasoned with sea salt and garlic sauce.
  • Top Side Wagyu: a cut of beef from the top round, which is a lean part of the cow with slow-cooked to perfection.
  • Alecatra: Cut from the top sirloin with a deep meaty flavor
  • Marinated Butterfish Fillet: Grilled fish marinated in sauce
  • Spicy Flank: Ignite your taste buds with our Spicy Flank, a fusion of bold and fiery flavors.
  • Leg of Lamb: New Zealand lamb leg well-seasoned sliced off the bone.
  • Honey Ham: Pork Ham marinated in slices with a sweet clove flavor.
  • Pork Belly: Tender and lean pork loin marinated and grilled to perfection.
  • Linguica: House made Brazilian style pork sausage with several spices.
  • Honey Ham Sausage: House made Brazilian style pork sausage made from ham and pork, flavored with honey and spices, offering a sweet and savory taste.
  • Chicken Bacon: Tender chicken fillets marinated and wrapped in bacon.
  • Chicken Hearts: Marinated chicken hearts perfectly seasoned.
  • River Prawns: Delight in the fresh catch of River Prawns, grilled to perfection.
  • Pineapple: Grilled sweet pineapple coated with cinnamon and brown sugar.
