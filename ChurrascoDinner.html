The Brazilian Style Churrasco Dinner

Churrasco is the name of the Brazilian way of having Gills, the premium cuts of meat, marinated with salt, and skewered to be grilled and served tableside in the rodizio style. At Pedro Churrascaria and Steakhouse, our Churrasco Buffet Dinner offers unlimited servings in the rodizio style. We carefully choose high-quality meats and prepare them with a Brazilian chef.
